## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I delete my account?**
Open the app and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data.

**How is my data used?**
Your email is stored securely for login only. Health and calendar data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers.

**How do I disconnect Google Calendar or Apple Health?**
Go to Settings and tap Disconnect next to Google Calendar, or adjust Health access in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health.

**I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
On the sign-in screen, tap Forgot Password and enter your email. We'll send you a reset link.
