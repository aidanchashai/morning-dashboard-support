# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 22, 2026**

## Information We Collect

**Account Data:** When you create an account, we collect your email address to authenticate you. Your email is stored securely on Supabase servers.

**Location:** We access your approximate location to display local weather. This data is not stored on our servers.

**Health & Fitness:** We access Apple Health data to display your fitness metrics. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

**Calendar:** We access your calendar to display today's events. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

## How We Use Your Information

- Email is used solely to authenticate your account
- Location is used solely to fetch local weather
- Health and calendar data is used solely to display information within the app

## Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

We use Supabase for authentication. Your email address is stored securely by Supabase.

## Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the app: go to **Settings → Account → Delete Account**. This removes your tasks, profile, and all stored data.
